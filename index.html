Main Text Content

In 1977, Billy's Beer was something of a household name.
President Jimmy Carter's brother, Billy Carter, was a renowned (and self-titled) "professional hillbilly", and was beloved across the country for his genuine & light demeanor.
Billy Beer is a brand of beer first made in the United States in July 1977, by the Falls City Brewing Company, and promoted by the aforementioned Billy. In October 1978, Falls City announced that it was closing after less than a year.

Billy Beer's history once catered to an exclusively rural crowd, but the rebirth focusses on general outdoorsmanship, and adventure seekers with a frontier spirit who want to
wet the whistle a bit after a long day of getting the camp ready.
